Patagonia

My Ultimate Bucket List Hike

Why Patagonia?

Stretching across the southern tips of Chile and Argentina, Patagonia is one of the most remote and breathtaking wilderness areas on Earth. Jagged granite peaks, electric blue glaciers, and howling winds define this legendary landscape. For hikers, it represents the ultimate challenge — and the ultimate reward.

The most famous route is the W Trek in Torres del Paine National Park, a 5-day journey through some of the most dramatic scenery imaginable. One day, I'll be there.

The iconic Torres del Paine, Patagonia's most recognizable landmark.

Top Hikes in Patagonia

Here are the must-do routes, organized by country:

  1. Chile – Torres del Paine
    • The W Trek (5 days)
    • The O Circuit (9 days, full loop)
    • Mirador Las Torres base hike (day trip)
  2. Argentina – Los Glaciares National Park
    • Laguna de los Tres (Mt. Fitz Roy views)
    • Laguna Torre
    • Perito Moreno Glacier walk
  3. Argentina – Tierra del Fuego
    • Coastal Trail in Tierra del Fuego National Park
    • Cerro Guanaco summit hike
